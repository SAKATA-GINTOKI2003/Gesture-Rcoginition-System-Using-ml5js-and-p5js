How to Use the Posture Detection

Step 1: Start the Camera

Allow access to your webcam. The camera feed will appear on the canvas, and the MoveNet model will start detecting body poses.

Step 2: Detect Keypoints

Key body points (e.g., shoulders, elbows, knees) are detected and highlighted with red circles when confidence is above 0.1.

Step 3: Connect the Skeleton

Green lines are drawn between keypoints to form a skeleton structure. The connection ensures each detected keypoint is part of a full-body pose.
