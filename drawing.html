How to Use the Gesture-Based Drawing Board

Step 1: Start the Camera

Allow access to your webcam when prompted. The camera feed will appear on the canvas, and the hand tracker will activate.

Step 2: Pinch to Draw

Bring your thumb and index finger close together (pinch gesture) to start drawing on the canvas. The black line follows your finger movement.

Step 3: Release to Stop

Separate your thumb and index finger to stop drawing. Red circles will appear on your fingertips to show tracking.
