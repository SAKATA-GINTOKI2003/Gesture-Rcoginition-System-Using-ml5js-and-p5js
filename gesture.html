Gesture Detection Types

Pointing Up Detection

Detects when the index finger is extended upwards. This gesture triggers navigation to the Pose Detection Page.

Two Fingers Up Detection

Identifies when two fingers are raised, serving as a placeholder gesture for future actions.

Three Fingers Up Detection

Identifies when Three fingers are raised, serving as a placeholder gesture for future actions.

Four Fingers Up Detection

Identifies when Four fingers are raised, serving as a placeholder gesture for future actions.

Closed Fist Detection

Detects a fully closed hand gesture, which can be mapped to perform custom actions.

Open Palm Detection

Recognizes when the palm is fully open with fingers spread apart, useful for additional gesture-based commands.
